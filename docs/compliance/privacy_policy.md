# Privacy Policy

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected
When you message our WhatsApp bot, we automatically collect:
- Encrypted phone number hash (SHA-256)
- Message content (your questions)
- Bot responses
- Timestamp and language preference

### What We Do NOT Collect
- Your real phone number
- Your name
- Student ID number
- Academic records
- Financial information
- Location data

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Purposes
- Answer your questions about college information
- Improve bot responses through quality monitoring
- Generate anonymous analytics for service improvement
- Detect and prevent abuse or spam

### Data Sharing
We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:
- **Twilio**: For message delivery (required by WhatsApp)
- **Groq**: For AI response generation (messages are processed but not stored)
- **Supabase**: For database storage (encrypted at rest)

## Your Rights

### GDPR Rights (International Students)
- **Right to Access**: Request a copy of your conversation history
- **Right to Erasure**: Request immediate deletion of your data
- **Right to Portability**: Receive your data in machine-readable format
- **Right to Object**: Opt-out of data processing

### How to Exercise Your Rights
Contact our Privacy Officer:
- Email: privacy@college.edu
- Phone: +1-XXX-XXX-XXXX
- Mail: [College Address]

## Data Security

### Technical Measures
- End-to-end encryption (HTTPS/TLS 1.3)
- Database encryption at rest (AES-256)
- Phone number hashing (SHA-256, irreversible)
- Regular security audits and penetration testing

### Organizational Measures
- Staff training on data protection
- Role-based access controls
- Incident response plan
- Data processing agreements with all vendors
